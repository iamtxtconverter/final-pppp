Dance Forms

Listed below are the most popular dance forms used as an exercise by people around the world

1.Zumba

"Zumba combines high energy and motivating music with unique moves and combinations that allow the Zumba participants to dance away their worries. It is based on the principle that a workout should be "FUN AND EASY TO DO" allowing Zumba participants to stick to the Zumba Fitness program and achieve long-term health benefits. Zumba is a “feel-happy” workout that is great for both the body and the mind.

2.Hip Hop

Suggested time: 1 hour.
A very intensive routine that focuses on the hips and waist.

3.Belly Dance

Suggested time: 30 minutes.
An Exotic dance form, which focuses on the hips, back, buttocks and abs.

4.Freestyle

Freestyle dancing allows for individual expression dancers are encouraged to create new moves and develop a personal style. freestyle dance classes are funky, modern and fun. It's all about freedom to express movement to the wide variety of music trends.

5.Pole Dance

Suggested time: 1 hour.
Pole dancing is a form of dance that is effective in toning muscles and shaping your body. However, this dance form is considered to be a bit difficult to master.
